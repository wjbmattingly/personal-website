This project was funded by the Harry Frank Guggenheim Fellowship. It focuses on providing open-source access to cleaned data from the South African Truth and Reconciliation Commission in South Africa. It also provides the tools and resources for analyzing over 3,000 transcripts and a dataset of 27,000 victims of human rights violations.
