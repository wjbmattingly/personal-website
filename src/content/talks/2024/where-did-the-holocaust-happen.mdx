Co-presented with Christine Liu (PhD Candidate, University of Maine) at the Lubartworld international conference _Quantifying the Holocaust. Classifying, Counting, Modeling: What contribution to Holocaust History?_ We discuss how machine learning models can be used to identify and locate places mentioned in Holocaust testimonies, and what the resulting geography tells us about the events described.
