A session for Yale's 2026 One IT Conference on how AI — especially vision-language models — is being used to read, transcribe, and structure handwritten archival material at scale. The talk draws on ongoing work with Yale's cultural heritage collections and discusses practical pipelines for digitizing handwritten documents.
