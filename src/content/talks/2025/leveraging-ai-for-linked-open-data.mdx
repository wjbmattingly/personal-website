Co-presented with Rob Sanderson at the Lorentz Center workshop _Enriching Digital Heritage with LLMs and Linked Open Data_, held in Leiden from 8–12 September 2025. We discuss how large language models can be combined with Linked Open Data to enrich cultural heritage metadata — with FAIR principles and practical recipes for institutions.
